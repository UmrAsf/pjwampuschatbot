Food Distribution Overview

Distribution occurs on planned weekly routes through West Campus.
Volunteers carry bags with food, water, and small essentials.

General process:
1. Meet at the designated starting point.
2. Organize food bags and supplies.
3. Walk the assigned route.
4. Offer items respectfully without pressure.
5. Log locations as requested by the team lead.
6. Return leftover supplies to storage.

The goal is reliable, consistent community support.
