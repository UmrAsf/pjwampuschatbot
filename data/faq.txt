FAQ

What does Project West Campus do?
- We distribute food and essentials to unhoused neighbors in West Campus.

Is this part of UT Austin?
- No. It is a student-led independent project.

How can I volunteer?
- Students can join food prep, distribution, logistics, or donation collection.

Does the project provide medical or housing services?
- No. We provide only food and essential items.

Is volunteering safe?
- Yes, volunteers work in groups and use safety guidelines.

Do you accept money?
- We currently focus on physical donations.
