Mission

Project West Campus is a student-led initiative focused on helping feed unhoused neighbors living in the West Campus area of Austin, Texas.
Our mission is to provide consistent, respectful, and human-centered food support through student volunteer efforts.
We emphasize compassion, reliability, and community responsibility.

Our work centers on:
- Distributing food and essentials
- Building trust with unhoused neighbors
- Supporting student volunteers with clear instructions and safety guidance
