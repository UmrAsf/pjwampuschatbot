Volunteer Roles

Food Prep Volunteers:
- Help assemble meals or care packages
- Portion food in sanitary, organized ways
- Bag and label items when needed

Route Walkers:
- Walk West Campus distribution routes
- Hand out supplies and meals
- Engage respectfully with neighbors

Drivers:
- Transport supplies or donated goods
- Assist with restocking if needed

Logistics Helpers:
- Keep track of inventory
- Restock bags
- Sort and organize donations
