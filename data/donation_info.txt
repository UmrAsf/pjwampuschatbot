Donation Information

We accept the following donations:
- Pre-packaged food
- Bottled water
- Hygiene items (soap, wipes, deodorant)
- Socks, gloves, and simple clothing
- Reusable bags

We cannot accept:
- Medication
- Perishable food from unknown sources
- Large furniture
- Items requiring climate-controlled storage

All donations go directly to unhoused neighbors during weekly routes.
